The 8 Essential Ingredients

Every piece in the Lunar Myth style is built from these core elements, layered in a specific order that creates depth, tactility, and emotional resonance.

01

Newspaper & Photo Collage

The skeleton. Torn clippings, vintage headlines, and photo slices form the figure's foundation.

02

Watercolor Bleed

The emotion. Soft jewel tones pool and flow across the paper layers, bringing life to the structure.

03

Obsidian & Stained Glass

The fracture. Sharp dark shards and iridescent mosaic represent the moment of breakage.

04

Golden Kintsugi

The repair. Visible gold lines transform fracture into beauty. Every crack is celebrated, not hidden.

05

Regenerative Botanicals

The growth. Moonflowers, iridescent ivy, and thorned shadow roses bloom from repair lines.

06

Embroidery & Sashiko

The tactility. Heavy beading, raised threadwork, and traditional Japanese stitch patterns create sensory depth.

07

Datamosh Glitch

The digital fracture. Controlled corruption adds modern impermanence to ancient craft.

08

Lunarpunk Glow

The atmosphere. Bioluminescent moonlight, chromatic aberration, and particle dust finish the piece.
